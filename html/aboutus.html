    About Us

    作为中国成立最早、最受欢迎的创意垂直网站，TOPYS立志打造一个多元、完备的泛创意知识产品系统和学习平台，试图成为“A Creative Institution”，希望借由创意的学习与激发，赋予人以最大价值及最高境界的成长

    我们以“Open Your Mind”为宗旨，自2003年成立以来，始终专注在全球创意、艺术、人文领域寻找最有质感的声音，将发掘和组织当代创意文化中最好的部分为己任，鼓励分享观念、想法、体验，以及对未知的想像，即为创意人提供一个学习交流、自由创造的平台，一个激发新观念的孵化器，一个以创意改变世界的桥梁。

    我们标榜的创意，不是一件商品，也不是一个产业，而是作用于所有产业，作用于人类感知世界，并以此探索未来的创新思维系统，是人类面向未来、延续发展的动力所在。中国正在改变，不只是产业转型，还开始探索产生“价值”的崭新方式，价值该如何创造和培养？ 1+1如何大于2？崭新的城市架构和全球网络意味着什么？观念变革的力量和思维方式的根本改变催生着一个全新的经济型态正在形成：以创意经济、美学经济、教育体验经济、博物馆经济为核心内容，以激发创造力为目标，尊重人、发展人、成就人的全新经济型态，一个创意的中国（from Made in China to Create in China），正在到来的路上。我们的目标正是借助全球性的创意思维资源，为“创意中国”提供知识储备与思想高地，为创意价值的被认可和提升贡献力量，为创意中国的起飞提供跑道。

    As one of the earliest and most popular creative vertical website in China, TOPYS is trying to become “A Creative Institution” which aspired to build a variety, completely creative knowledge system and learning platform. We hope creator could achieve maximum value through creative emulation and inspiration.

    We take “Open Your Mind” as a purpose and focus on searching the most extraordinary work in Creative, Art and Humanities since 2003. Our mission is to dig deep inside the creative culture to show the best part for creator and encouraging them to share ideas, thoughts, experiences and imagination of unknown. We are a platform, an incubator, a bridge for creator to change the world!

    The meaning of “Creative” in TOPYS is never a merchandise nor an industry. Rather, it is an innovative thinking system for human to apperceive the world and explore the future and it is also a power for human to face the future and continue development. China is now changing, “How to create value? How to make 1+1>2? What does the new urban structure and global network mean to China?” are the hottest topics. A new economic formation is forming by the change of wisdom and thought: Taking the Creative economy, Aesthetic economy, Educational experience economy and Museum economy as the core and aiming to stimulate creativity. It is time to go from “Made in China” to “Create in China”.Our objective is to provide knowledge reserve for “Creative China” and to make contribution to the recognition and improvement of “Creative Value” with the help of global creative thinking resources.
